Part VI: IOS STDIO

     

The Standard I/O (STDIO) interface provided by the I/O Subsystem (IOS) in Palm OS® lets programmers use familiar Posix-like functions to access Palm OS device drivers. This part covers this API, as well as other IOS APIs for managing drivers.
