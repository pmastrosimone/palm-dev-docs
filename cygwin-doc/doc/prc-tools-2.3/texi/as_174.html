8.2.3.2 Register Names

The 32 integer registers are refered to as `$n' or `$rn'. In addition, registers 15, 28, 29, and 30 may be refered to by the symbols `$fp', `$at', `$gp', and `$sp' respectively.

The 32 floating-point registers are refered to as `$fn'.
